Services

Neurology

We provide advanced neurosurgical interventions for conditions such as brain tumors, spinal cord injuries, hydrocephalus, and peripheral nerve disorders, utilizing minimally invasive techniques whenever possible to optimize patient outcomes.

Eyes care

We offer specialized treatments for glaucoma, including medication management, laser therapy, and surgical options like trabeculectomy or minimally invasive glaucoma surgery (MIGS), aimed at reducing intraocular pressure and preserving vision.

Pulomonary

We offer advanced pulmonary function testing to assess lung capacity, airflow, and gas exchange, helping to diagnose and monitor respiratory conditions and structured exercise training, breathing exercises, and education to help patients .

Cardiology

Our cardiologists provide expert management of acute cardiac conditions, such as heart attacks, arrhythmias, and heart failure, offering timely interventions, including angioplasty, stent placement, cardioversion, and advanced heart failure therapies and reduce the risk of future complications.

Dental Care

Our dental care services focus on maintaining and improving oral health, providing preventive, restorative, and cosmetic treatments for patients of all ages and Our cosmetic dentistry services include teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers, allowing patients to enhance the appearance of their teeth.

Hepatology

Our hepatologists provide specialized care for liver diseases such as hepatitis, fatty liver disease, autoimmune liver diseases, cirrhosis, liver cancer, and genetic liver disorders.
